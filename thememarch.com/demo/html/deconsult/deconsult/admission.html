At Grait International College School, we are committed to providing a nurturing, safe, and academically stimulating environment where every child can grow, learn, and excel. We welcome applications from prospective students into our Creche, Nursery, Primary, Junior Secondary, and Senior Secondary sections. Our admission process is designed to ensure that every child is placed in the appropriate class level and receives the support needed to achieve academic and personal success.

Our Creche and Nursery section provides a warm and caring environment where young learners begin their educational journey. Through play-based learning, creative activities, and guided instruction, children develop essential social, emotional, and cognitive skills that prepare them for future academic success.

In our Primary School, pupils receive a strong academic foundation while developing confidence, creativity, discipline, and problem-solving skills through a balanced and engaging curriculum.

The Junior Secondary School section serves as a bridge between primary and senior secondary education, helping students strengthen their academic abilities, leadership qualities, and personal development.

At the Senior Secondary School level, students are prepared for higher education and future careers through rigorous academic training, career guidance, character development, and preparation for external examinations.

With a strong focus on academic excellence, character development, leadership, creativity, and innovation, we equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in an ever-changing world. Our experienced teachers, modern learning approaches, and supportive learning environment help every student build confidence and achieve their full potential both inside and outside the classroom.

Whether your child is beginning their educational journey in our Creche and Nursery section or joining our Primary, Junior Secondary, or Senior Secondary School, Grait International College School offers a solid foundation for lifelong learning and success. We invite parents and guardians to explore our admission opportunities and become part of a community dedicated to excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement.

Application Process

At Grait International College School, our admission process is simple, transparent, and designed to ensure that every child is placed in the appropriate learning environment. Parents and guardians are encouraged to follow the steps below to complete the admission process successfully.

Step 1 - Obtain Admission Form

Visit the school campus or contact the Admissions Office to obtain the official admission form. Ensure that all required information is completed accurately.

Step 2 - Submit Required Documents

Submit the completed admission form along with all required supporting documents, including birth certificate, passport photographs, and previous academic records where applicable.

Step 3 - Assessment & Interview

Applicants may be required to participate in an entrance assessment and interview to determine appropriate class placement.

Step 4 - Admission Review

Submitted applications and assessment results will be reviewed by the Admissions Committee for consideration and approval.

Step 5 - Offer of Admission

Successful applicants will receive an official admission offer and enrollment instructions from the school.

Step 6 - Registration & Enrollment

Parents are required to complete the registration process and payment of approved fees to secure the student's admission.

We encourage parents and guardians to begin the admission process early, as available spaces may be limited in some classes. Our Admissions Team is always available to provide guidance and support throughout the process.
